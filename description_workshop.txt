[h2]TLDR[/h2]

- Adds tooltips detailing stats of hooks and wings
- Displays a comparison to your equipped hook and wings
- Generic mod support for most stats, manual support for many popular content mods
- Mod calls provided for other modders to set their own stats
- Disables this mods' tooltips for vanilla items when Calamity is enabled as Calamity adds its own
- Client side
- Highly configurable
